Welcome to the S.E.P.I.A. app for browser, iOS and Android! :-) In this little tutorial you will learn the basics to get started with your personal assistant.

Here is an overview of the controls:
Some buttons have a long-press action assigned as well like the shortcuts (settings) and the mic (audio-input reset).

If you want to ask S.E.P.I.A. a question just press the mic or type in the chat field, send and wait for your answer. Sometimes when you encounter problems with the mic and it gets stuck in "gray"-mode you can release it with a long-press.

Tutorial - Page 2 of 3

When you start S.E.P.I.A. you usually end up in your start-screen (sometimes called 'my-view'). This view will show you personal info like upcoming reminders or recommendations based on the time of day. You can fill this screen with results you get from from chatting with S.E.P.I.A. (more about this later).

There are 2 more main views to interact with S.E.P.I.A., the chat view and the results view. You can reach them with the control on the top or by swiping from the edges of the screen (similar to iOS safari navigation).
